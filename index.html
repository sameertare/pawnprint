OpenFile

A toolkit for chess parents, players & organizers. Seven tools, each its own app — all running locally with Stockfish 18.

📊

Performance Analysis

Upload chess.com / lichess PGNs and get a deep report: openings, phase strengths, tactics, blunders, loss patterns, W/D/L by time control, and a puzzle-based training plan. The main player is auto-detected — no picking required. Save as Markdown and grow it over time.

Open analyzer →
📡

Live & Engine

Watch a live lichess game move-by-move with instant engine feedback on every move, or set up any position (FEN or click-to-move) and get Stockfish 18's best move, evaluation and principal variation.

Open live board →
🏆

Swiss Pairings

Paste a tournament roster or upload a file, then run a full Swiss tournament: automatic round pairings (color balancing, no rematches, byes), result entry, and live standings with Buchholz & Sonneborn-Berger tiebreaks.

Open pairings →
🌳

Opening Explorer

Turn your own PGNs into a branching opening tree — or scout an opponent's account the same way. See how often each move gets played and the practical score from there, drilling in move by move, with every underlying game listed.

Explore your openings →
⚖

Compare Reports

Upload two saved Performance Analysis reports — last month vs. today, before vs. after a training push — and see every metric side by side with the delta highlighted: accuracy, blunder rates, openings, patterns, all of it.

Compare two reports →

USCF Rating Estimator

Estimate your new US Chess rating after an event: enter your current rating, score, prior games and up to 15 opponent ratings to get your new rating, performance rating, K-factor and bonus points.
